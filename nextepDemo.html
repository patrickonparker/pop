I put together a quick scenario to help us visualize the challenges we're having with Compass, and put together an interactive mockup for a tool that could be bundled into myNextep to adddress those challenges. This is just brainstorming, I'm looking for feedback to keep the conversation moving forward.

Scenario

  1. I am setting up a revenue center which uses the common 'Compass 3' theme, so I specify that under the 'Theme' field.
  2. It is a revenue center on the Intel campus, so I specify the Intel sub-theme in the 'Theme Variant' feld.
  3. This specifc revenue center has an oldschool swipey card reader instead of the new chip readers, so I am going to override the default chip reader animation with a swipey reader animation, called swipeyCard.gif. Since this is revene-center-specific change, I'll put the change in the custom css field at the bottom.

Theme Builder
