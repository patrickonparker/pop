Values

I am deeply invested in the success of my clients and am sincerely committed to offering the best service at the lowest price possible. My desire is to counsel and equip startups to make the best use of web technologies to achieve the potential that I see in them.

Web development is a passion and a responsibility that I take very seriously. I invest a lot of time and energy fine-tuning every detail of a website to communicate each clients' vision, brand and values in a way that will convincingly speak to and engage their target audience.

This is not my day job and my time is limited. When I accept a job, it is because I personally believe in and respect the people I am serving and the vision of their project. I insist on meeting my clients in person whenever possible and often grow to think of them as friends.
