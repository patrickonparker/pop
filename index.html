Values

I create beautiful and affordable websites because I believe in my clients and am deeply invested in their success. My desire is to counsel and equip my clients to realize their goals by making the best possible use of web technologies. I invest a lot of energy into effectively communicating each client's vision, brand and values. No two sites ever look alike.

I offer the lowest rates in the industry for nonprofit and for-good businesses so that those doing good work can put as much of their time and resources as possible into their cause. I only take clients whose work I personally believe in and who I feel good about supporting with my time, talent and energy. I insist on meeting my clients in person whenever possible and often grow to think of them as friends.
